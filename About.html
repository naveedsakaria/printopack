About Us


The Journey Starts in 2005, with the Name of PRINT O PACK, A Offset Printing Press Unit at Pakistan Chowk Karachi. Since then we have been growing to develop our vision of creating an PVC Bag Manufacturing unit which got it face in the mid of 2009.

We still continue our dedication to develop high quality products, innovating packaging solutions & improving customer satisfaction. We provide premium Packaging Solutions for exceptional value to individuals and businesses in the most efficient, profitable and sustainable way. We offer a complete range of Packaging Solutions including Offset Printing, Flaxo Printing & PVC Bag making to Individuals and Businesses all over Pakistan.

We Specialize in PVC Bag making Solid Fancy Boxes, point of purchase PVC Bags, Papar Bags, Loop Handlde Bags, Shoppers, BOPP Bags, etc.

We Position our services as a regional player in quality printing & packaging and consumer products. We tend to improve on contemporary basis including cost, quality, service, speed of delivery and mobilization. our focus is to invest in technology and human resource to effectively meet the day to day challenges of the industry. our aim is to develop healthy releationships with all our stakeholders based on sustainable cooperation, upholding ethical values and creating satisfaction.
